About APCOER

Anantrao Pawar College of Engineering & Research

Established in 1999, APCOER is committed to providing quality technical education and fostering innovation. Located in a serene environment, our campus offers state-of-the-art facilities that support holistic growth and academic excellence.

At APCOER, we aim to nurture leaders who will contribute to the technological advancement of society through ethical practices and continuous learning.

Visit Official Website

Academic Excellence

APCOER offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines, approved by AICTE and affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University.

Research Opportunities

Our well-equipped laboratories and research centers provide students with opportunities to explore and innovate in their respective fields.

Student Life

APCOER promotes a vibrant campus life with numerous clubs, cultural events, sports activities, and technical competitions.

Industry Connections

We maintain strong ties with leading industries to ensure that our students gain practical exposure and better placement opportunities.

Academic Programs

Computer Engineering

Focusing on software development, computer architecture, and information technology.

Information Technology

Covering data management, networking, and system integration.

Mechanical Engineering

Emphasizing design, manufacturing, and thermal engineering principles.

Civil Engineering

Dealing with structural design, construction, and environmental aspects.

Electronics & Telecommunications

Focusing on electronic systems, communication networks, and signal processing.

Artificial Intelligence & Data Science

Exploring machine learning, data analytics, and intelligent systems.
